01 / The concept

A tea bar
you run.

We handed the bar over. Walk up, grab a cup, load it like a sundae from twenty toppings, then fill it from every tea on tap. Zero judgment about that double pudding move — we seal it, you take the credit.

It's bubble tea as choose-your-own-adventure, plus a kitchen out back making the kind of Taiwanese comfort food — and a shaved snow counter — that turns a study session into a dinner.

"It's the only place where ordering takes five minutes and I still want it to take longer."

03 / How it works

Three steps,
one golden rule.

01

Purchase a cup

Pay first at the register. Small or regular — that empty cup is your ticket to the bar.

02

Load & fill

Mochi, taro, boba, aloe, egg pudding, lychee jelly — twenty toppings in. Then your drink of choice. We're not the topping police.

03

Get it sealed

Bring it back to the cashier. We seal it tight, you walk out looking like a pro. Class dismissed.

!

No free refills

House rule, hand-painted on the sign. Tray goes back to the trash station. We love you anyway.
